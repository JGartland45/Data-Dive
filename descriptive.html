Descriptive Data Analytics

What is Descriptive Data Analytics?

Descriptive data analytics involves the examination and interpretation of historical data to gain insights into past trends and events. It focuses on summarizing data, identifying patterns, and providing a clear picture of what has happened in the past. This information is valuable for making informed decisions, understanding performance, and creating reports.
